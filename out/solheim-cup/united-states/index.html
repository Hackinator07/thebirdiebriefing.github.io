United States Solheim Cup Team

Team USA's Solheim Cup squad is 12 LPGA players: a mix of automatic qualifiers, Rolex Rankings spots, and captain's picks.

Team Selection Process

Automatic Qualification: Top 7 players on the U.S. Solheim Cup points list at the end of the qualifying period.
Rolex Rankings: Top 2 players on the Rolex Women's World Golf Rankings not already qualified.
Captain's Picks: 3 players selected by the U.S. Solheim Cup Team Captain.
